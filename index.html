ABOUT

Our Blooms was founded in honor of Lily Smith having a love of flowers and bringing joy to the world

Our story began in 1975, when Lily Smith opened a small flower stand on the corner of Main Street. What started as a passion project quickly blossomed into something much bigger—a place where people could find beauty, comfort, and joy through the language of flowers.

Nearly five decades later, we continue Lily's legacy by creating arrangements that speak to the heart. Each bouquet tells a story, whether it's celebrating love, offering comfort, or simply brightening someone's day.

We believe in the transformative power of flowers and the connections they help us create. From weddings to everyday moments, we're honored to be part of your most meaningful celebrations.

Today, Our Blooms is more than a flower shop—it's a community. A place where artistry meets nature, and where every stem is selected with intention and care.

Every bouquet carries with it a dedication to quality and beauty that honors Lily's original vision.

From botanical beginnings, discover how we've craft beauty with each arrangement and how our passion for flowers continues to bloom.
