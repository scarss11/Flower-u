ABOUT

SportStyle was founded in 2010 by former athlete Maria Rodriguez, combining performance technology with contemporary athletic style

Our story began in 2010, when former collegiate athlete Maria Rodriguez identified a gap in the athletic wear market. She wanted performance gear that didn't sacrifice style—clothing that could take you from the gym to casual outings without compromising on function or fashion.

What started as a small boutique in downtown Portland has grown into a trusted brand for athletes at every level. We believe that what you wear should enhance your performance, not hinder it, while making you look and feel your best.

Every piece in our collection is tested by real athletes in real conditions. We work with textile engineers and professional trainers to ensure our fabrics provide the perfect balance of compression, breathability, and durability.

Today, SportStyle is more than an athletic wear company—it's a community of fitness enthusiasts, professional athletes, and everyday people who value quality, performance, and style in equal measure.

Every garment carries with it our commitment to innovation and quality that honors Maria's original vision of performance meeting style.

From technical innovations to sustainable practices, discover how we're pushing the boundaries of athletic wear and helping athletes perform at their best.
